ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was founded in 2025 by Ms. Vasanthi, inspired by a love for quality coffee and a welcoming atmosphere. What started as a small idea quickly became a cozy neighborhood space known for its charm and warm service.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we offer fresh, made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, seasonal salads, flavorful sides, and more. Every item is prepared with care, using quality ingredients to give you a simple yet satisfying experience.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Vasanthi

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: 15 Adr street, 5015, NY
